## The 4 packaging rules (apply to every clip)

1. **Payoff-as-hook.** Lead with the reframe/payoff in the first 1.5s, then the minimal setup. Don't make the viewer wait for the point.
2. **Plain-English hook on screen first.** Put the modern-English hook on screen before the KJV lands. Let *"thou mighty man of valour"* arrive as the payoff *inside* the clip, not the cold open.
3. **The bridge.** Every caption ends with one plain line pointing to the full episode. The linked episode should open cold on the teaching (not the 90-second intro).
4. **Serialize.** Every caption carries the series line: *"I'm walking through every name God calls Himself — follow so you don't miss one."*
