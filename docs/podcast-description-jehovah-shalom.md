## Episode description (copy-paste)

```
Gideon was hiding in a winepress — and you don't thresh wheat in a winepress. That one detail tells you everything about the fear he was living in.

In this episode we put Jehovah Shalom under the scope: the name that means "The Lord Is Peace," and the backwards truth Gideon discovered — that peace comes BEFORE the battle, not after it. Before the army, before the fleece, before the 300, God gives a frightened man a name for who He is. Not the peace that arrives once the fight is over. The Presence that carries you into it.

This is Part 1 of Gideon (Judges 6), and one name in our series "What God Calls Himself." Follow the show so you don't miss a single name.

In this episode:
• Why Gideon was really hiding in a winepress — and what it reveals about fear

• "Surely I will be with thee" — the whole battle plan in four words
```
