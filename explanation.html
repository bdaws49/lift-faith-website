Frequently Asked Questions

How often will I receive verses?
You choose! Select between 2-6 notifications per day based on what works best for your schedule and spiritual rhythm.
How long does the program last?
Your initial subscription is for one month. After 30 days, you'll have the option to continue receiving encouragement for as long as you'd like.
Is my information private?
Absolutely. Your struggles, goals, and prayer requests are completely confidential. Prayer requests shared with the community are anonymous.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes! You can upgrade from Basic to Premium or adjust your notification preferences at any time.
What translation of the Bible do you use?
We primarily use the NKJV (New King James Version), but may include other trusted translations as well.
